About this Mapplication

This mapplications serves to uplift and share the lived experiences of UCLA students who have been impacted by housing insecurity or unaffordability. This is a space where UCLA students can share their experiences with issues related to insecurity or unaffordability, and a place where community members can learn from their experiences. If you'd like to share your story, please press the button below.
