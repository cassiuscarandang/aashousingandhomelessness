About this Mapplication

This mapplication was created to uplift and share the lived experiences of UCLA students who have been impacted by housing insecurity or unaffordability. While past studies have gathered important findings around this issue, there is currently a lack of research that systematically collects student voices on their experience with housing and how it may impact student life. Through taking the survey, this is a space where UCLA students can share their experiences, and a place where community members can learn about the impacts, barriers, and resources available to students. If you'd like to share your story, please press the button below.

Mission Statement

Housing 4 All acknowledges the personal harships that many underserved low-income students face in large instituions. One of those many hardships is acquiring a sense of security and support when it comes to battling housing insecurity and non affordability. Our mission is to crtically take the time to ensure that every student's voices can be heard through their experiences and mitigate their problems by utilizing this information to understand others experiences in which can help make better informed decisions.
